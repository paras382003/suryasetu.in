Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our digital services.

Is SuryaSetu a government website? +

No, SuryaSetu is a private consultancy and digital facilitation center. We are not affiliated with the government. We charge a service fee for assisting you with online forms, documentation, and filings to save you time and effort.

Is my personal data and document safe? +

Yes, absolutely. We follow a strict data privacy policy. Your documents are used solely for the purpose of your application and are deleted from our systems after the service is completed. We never share your data with third parties.

What if my application gets rejected? +

If an application is rejected due to an error on our part, we will re-apply for you free of cost or provide a full refund of our service charge. However, rejections due to ineligibility or incorrect documents provided by you are not refundable.

How do I make the payment? +

We accept all digital payment modes including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and Credit/Debit Cards. Payment links are shared securely via WhatsApp or Email after document verification.

How will I get my receipt or admit card? +

Once your form is submitted successfully, we will instantly share the PDF acknowledgement, receipt, or admit card with you via WhatsApp and Email. You can take a printout at your convenience.

Do you provide services on Sundays? +

Yes, our digital support team is active 7 days a week from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM to assist you with urgent form fillings and queries.
